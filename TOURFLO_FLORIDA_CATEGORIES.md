## 4. PRICING STRATEGY

### 4.1 Dynamic Pricing Factors

**Seasonal Multipliers:**
- Peak Season (Dec-Apr): 1.2-1.5x base price
- Shoulder Season (May, Oct-Nov): 1.0x base price
- Summer (Jun-Sep): 0.8-1.0x base price
- Hurricane Season discount: 0.7-0.9x (Aug-Oct)

**Time of Day:**
- Sunrise/Early Bird: 0.9x (incentive)
- Mid-Day: 1.0x base
- Sunset/Prime: 1.2-1.3x premium
- Night Tours: 1.1-1.2x

**Group Size:**
- Solo: 1.5x per person
- Couple: 1.0x per person
- Small Group (3-4): 0.9x per person
- Large Group (5+): 0.8x per person
- Private Charter: Fixed rate

**Booking Window:**
- Same Day: 1.2x (premium for availability)
- 1-3 Days: 1.0x
- 4-7 Days: 0.95x
- 8-14 Days: 0.9x
- 15+ Days: 0.85x (early bird discount)

---

### 4.2 Commission Structure (TourFlo Platform)

**Standard Tiers:**
- **Starter (0-50 bookings/month):** 12% commission
- **Growth (51-200 bookings/month):** 10% commission
- **Pro (201-500 bookings/month):** 8% commission
- **Enterprise (501+ bookings/month):** 6% commission

**Comparison to Competitors:**
- Viator: 20-30% commission
